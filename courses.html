EE 005 - Introduction to Electrical Engineering

Fundamental concepts in electrical engineering including basic circuit analysis, components, and design principles.

EE 021 - Digital Systems

Introduction to digital logic, Boolean algebra, combinational and sequential circuits, and digital system design.

EE 101 - Circuit Analysis

Advanced circuit analysis techniques including AC/DC circuits, network theorems, and frequency response.

EE 104 - Electronics

Semiconductor devices, amplifiers, operational amplifiers, and electronic circuit design and analysis.

EE 105 - Signals and Systems

Signal processing fundamentals, Fourier analysis, Laplace transforms, and system analysis.

EE 130 - Control Systems

Control theory, feedback systems, stability analysis, and controller design for dynamic systems.
