What We Provide

We provide workshops, tutoring, and projects. Main priority is on EE students and current engineering projects, helping out with classes in EE 005, 021, 101, 104, 105, 130

Workshops

Hands-on technical workshops to enhance your engineering skills and knowledge. Our workshops cover topics ranging from circuit design to programming fundamentals.

Tutoring

Peer-to-peer tutoring sessions for challenging engineering courses. Get help from experienced students who have successfully completed the courses you're taking.

Projects

Real-world engineering projects to build your portfolio and experience. Work on practical applications that demonstrate your skills to potential employers.

Study Groups

Collaborative study sessions where students work together to master difficult concepts and prepare for exams.

Industry Connections

Networking opportunities with industry professionals and alumni to help you understand career paths and job opportunities.

Research Opportunities

Access to research projects and mentorship opportunities that can enhance your academic and professional development.
