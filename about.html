About Us

We are a student organization on campus that helps Electrical Engineering and Computer Science students with more professional development, research projects, and leading experiences.

Our Mission

To empower electrical engineering and computer science students through professional development, research opportunities, and leadership experiences that prepare them for successful careers in technology.

Our Vision

To be the premier student organization at UC Merced that bridges the gap between academic learning and real-world engineering applications.

Our Values

Excellence in engineering education, innovation in problem-solving, collaboration among peers, and service to the engineering community.
