About Us

We are a student organization on campus that helps Electrical Engineering and Computer Science students with more professional development, research projects, and leading experiences.

What We Provide

We provide workshops, tutoring, and projects. Main priority is on EE students and current engineering projects, helping out with classes in EE 005, 021, 101, 104, 105, 130
